How to Play

Step One: Once you press play game, you'll be prompted with some grids asking you to choose a point value. Points range from 100 to 1000 and correspond to difficulty.
Step Two: Once you choose a specific point value you will be brought to a question & answer page. You get up to three tries and you do not have to phrase your answers as questions (the way Jeapordy usually works).
Step Three: After your three tries are up, we will provide a link to a Google website relating to your question. Click on the link if you want to learn more about your topic!
